Perspectives

We will continue doing science education for the iGEM competition. Through our project, our team has sensitized students on the integration and the acceptance of biotechnologies.

Because promoting molecular biology and synthetic biology is essential, a partnership was formed between Europabio and our team ! The goal of this alliance is to communicate about green biotechnology in exchange for financial support (click to see the invoice and the collaboration report with Europabio). It is an honor for us, to continue promoting and sharing an innovative and dynamic environment for the agricultural biotech and promote the communication about synthetic biology.





We also met the mayor of Paris , Mrs.Hidalgo Anne, to organise events in French schools for children and universities. We will have to prepare further conferences and activities to encourage society to learn more about science, research, and biotechnology. These two future perspectives will encourage the general population to learn more about synthetic biology and discover all the amazing opportunities that it offers!
